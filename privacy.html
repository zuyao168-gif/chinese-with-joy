Privacy, in plain words.

Effective July 7, 2026 · applies to chinesewithjoy.net and the China Survival Kit iOS app.

This website

What we collect: your email address — only if you type it into the free-guide form. It's processed by MailerLite and used to send you the 30-phrase pack and an occasional tip from Joy. Every email has an unsubscribe link — unsubscribing stops all mail immediately; if you also want your address erased from MailerLite's records, email us and we'll delete it.

What we don't do: no ad trackers, no analytics cookies, no selling or sharing your address with anyone. One third-party script loads on the homepage: MailerLite's form embed, which powers the signup box and may set functional cookies of its own. We measure aggregate traffic with Cloudflare Web Analytics — it is cookie-less and collects no personal identifiers. The site is otherwise static, served by GitHub Pages, which keeps standard server logs (IP, request time) per GitHub's privacy statement.

Interactive demos (like the tail-number toy) run entirely in your browser — nothing you type is stored or sent anywhere.

The China Survival Kit app

Everything you enter in the app — your emergency card, phone digits, allergies, favorites — is stored on your device only. There are no accounts, no server, and the app works fully offline. Purchases and restore are handled by Apple; we never see your payment details.

Questions or deletion requests

Email hello@chinesewithjoy.net and we'll sort it out — usually within a couple of days.
